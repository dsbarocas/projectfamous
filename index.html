Make-Me-Famous is a provider of social media services where we provide you the resources necessary to become famous and become the best you, you can be. We offer social media services that ensure your outreach is heard and seen by all.

Background Information of Make-Me-Famous

Make-Me-Famous was started by a group of teenagers aiming to be as popular as their friends in school. In 2015, this company aimed to give others the opportunity to be famous and be popular. David Barocas, CEO, had given us the following statement:

"I used to watch so many students wander around the halls so down and upset. I decided to figure out why. I realized that they didn't have snapchat friends. That's when I knew that social media was consuming people and it was time to step in and aide people who needed help." - David Barocas, 2015
